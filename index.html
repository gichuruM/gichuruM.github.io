Our Home

Comprehensive Care & Amenities

A home designed for comfort, safety, and peace of mind.

24/7 Qualified Care

Our highly qualified and continuously trained caregivers are available 24/7, providing medication management and assistance with all activities of daily living (ADLs).

DSHS Approved Accessibility

Our DSHS approved home features entry/exit ramps, wide hallways, and wheelchair-accessible spaces to ensure safety, mobility, and easy evacuation if needed.

Comfort & Mobility

Residents enjoy our relaxing outdoor deck and benefit from mobility equipment, including electric beds, sit-to-stand chairs, walkers, and wheelchairs.

Prime, Quiet Location

Located on a quiet cul-de-sac, our home is less than 1 mile from an emergency station and just 5 miles from Good Samaritan Hospital.

Transportation Services

We provide and arrange transportation for doctor's appointments, social outings, shopping, and other essential errands for our residents.

Flexible Payment Options

We proudly accept both Medicaid-paying clients and private pay residents, offering competitive charges for our comprehensive care.

Our Independent Living option is perfect for seniors who are self-sufficient but desire the security and social benefits of a community environment. We provide a safe and engaging atmosphere where residents can live life to the fullest.

Residents enjoy all home amenities, including communal dining and social activities, while maintaining the privacy of their own space. It's the ideal balance of freedom and peace of mind.

Assisted Living

Assisted Living at Gentle Care provides a balance of independence and support. We offer personalized assistance with daily activities (ADLs) such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and medication management.

Our 24/7 staff is always available to lend a helping hand, ensuring every resident receives the care they need while being encouraged to stay active and engaged in our community.

Memory Care

Our specialized Memory Care program offers a safe, secure, and nurturing environment for residents with Alzheimer's or other forms of dementia. Our home is designed to be safe and easy to navigate.

Our staff is continuously trained in the latest dementia care techniques, focusing on person-centered care. We provide structured activities that promote engagement, reduce agitation, and support cognitive function.

Respite Care

Respite Care provides a temporary, short-term stay for seniors. This service is a valuable resource for family caregivers who may need a break, have to travel, or are recovering from their own illness.

Your loved one will receive all the same amenities and care as our full-time residents, including 24/7 supervision, medication management, and social activities, all within a safe and welcoming environment.

Hospice Care

We partner with local hospice agencies to provide compassionate end-of-life care. Our focus is on providing comfort, dignity, and peace for residents in a familiar and loving home environment.

Our trained staff works closely with the hospice team to manage pain and symptoms, providing emotional and spiritual support to both the resident and their family during this difficult time.
